---
title: On Simplicity
date: 2026-04-18
category: thoughts
summary: Why simple tools tend to outlast complex ones.
---

There's a pattern I keep noticing: the tools that last are the ones that do less. Plain text outlives every proprietary format. A shell script outlives the framework it was written to replace.

Complexity is easy to add and hard to remove. Every feature is a promise you have to keep forever.

> Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.

The best software is the kind you forget is there.
